Cardano is a decentralised platform that will allow complex programmable transfers of value in a secure and scalable fashion. Cardano is the first blockchain to be built in the highly secure Haskell programming language.

Conceived and developed by a global team of researchers, academics and engineers, Cardano aims to bring sophistication and maturity to blockchain technology, and build a platform that is secure and stable enough to be adopted globally.

Here you can learn more about the protocol, the people behind it, and the role of The Cardano Foundation in supporting the effort and building the Cardano community.
